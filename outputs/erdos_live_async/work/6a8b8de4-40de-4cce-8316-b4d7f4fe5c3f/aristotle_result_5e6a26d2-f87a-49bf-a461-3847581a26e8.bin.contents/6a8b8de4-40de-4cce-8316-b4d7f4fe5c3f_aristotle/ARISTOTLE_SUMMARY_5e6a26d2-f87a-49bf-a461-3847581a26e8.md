## Boundary Variant: Counterexample Found

**Result**: `PowTripleSet 2 4 8` is formally proved to NOT be d-complete (`boundary_counterexample`).

**Mechanism**: When all three generators share a common prime factor (here 2), the multiplicative semigroup collapses to a chain under divisibility. Antichains in a chain are singletons, so antichain sums are just individual elements вЂ” which cannot cover all large integers.

This shows the coprimality hypothesis is *necessary*, not merely sufficient.
